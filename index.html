TIC TAC TOE

Objective: Learned iOS MVVM Architecture, Swift. The tic-tac-toe game is for two players. One player plays X and the other plays O. The players take turns placing their marks on a grid of three-by-three cells. If a given player gets three marks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, then that player wins the game. when the user shakes his/her phone, they can undo their move. At the top of the page, display the score of each player. On the top-left and right sides of the screen, display the score for X, and O, respectively. A swipe gesture on the screen will reset the game to a “new game”.
